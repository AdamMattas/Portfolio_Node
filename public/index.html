About Me

Greetings!

My name is Adam Mattas.

I develop masterful websites and applications to serve companies and professionals. All phases of a project are enjoyable to me. Innovating the initial concept, applying the final details, and everything in between.

I am a graduate of the Rutgers University full-stack certification program. I excelled throughout the challenging program while providing 1:1 tutoring sessions for new students and fellow classmates in need of academic support. I was aslo proud to be the Lead Developer for all assigned team projects, charged with maintaining workflow, coaching, and fulfilling deadline requirements.

I have an inherent talent for arriving at aesthetically pleasing solutions that enhance the end user experience and funtionality of an application. I take pride in what I do and continually work to improve my skills and grow as a developer. The challenges that are presented while coding can be daunting. However, overcoming the obstacles I have encountered has been more rewarding than I could have imagined.

Thank you for taking the time view my portfolio. Please feel free to contact me or connect with me through any of the resources available on this site.
